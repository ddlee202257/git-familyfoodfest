Family Food Fest Atlanta

This multicultural food festival celebration, which debut in 2015, is a 501.c3 non-profit organization. Atlanta Culinary Charities, established in 2017, produces this event each year on Father's Day to celebrate men who love and support their families and to showcase the culinary skills of chefs, cooks, and dads. This event brings families together in a fun and energetic atmosphere. We have lots of chefs, cooks, caterers, restaurants and every day dads displaying their culinary skills and talents. We also have female chefs on hand as well.

At this event you will get a wide variety of foods to sample throughout the day. The Family Food Fest Atlanta festival is for everyone and is open to the entire family. The idea of this family-oriented event is to enjoy some great cuisine from well-know chefs, cooks, caterers, and restaurants. With an "all-you-can-eat" admission price you will be able to taste delicious appetizers, entrees, desserts, and beverages. Also, there will be activities available through the day including live entertainment, chef demonstrations, kid-zone, silent auction, panel discussions, FREE health screenings, and much more.
